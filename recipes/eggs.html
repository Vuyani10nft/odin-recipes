Heaven in you mouth

This is what we will be making

It is simple but effective

Ingredients

  • Stove
  • Pan
  • Oil
  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt
  • Love (Secret Ingredient)

Steps

  1. Put stove on Electricity
  2. Switch stove on
  3. Put pan on stove
  4. Put oil on pan
  5. Break eggs into Pan
  6. Season with salt
  7. Flip Eggs
  8. Plate
  9. (Make sure to do it all with Love)
